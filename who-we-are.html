Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Quick answers about U S AUTO MANAGEMENT compliance, deliverability, and getting started.

Is automotive texting compliant with industry regulations?

Yes. U S AUTO MANAGEMENT includes consent capture, dealership opt-in protocols, HELP/STOP keywords, quiet hours, and audit logs to support TCPA/CTIA automotive compliance standards.

Do you handle 10DLC registration for dealerships?

We guide dealerships through brand and campaign registration and handle carrier submissions to maximize message delivery for automotive communications.

Can I integrate with dealership CRM or DMS?

Absolutely. Use our automotive-specific connectors or REST API to sync customer data, vehicle information, and service history from your dealership systems.

How is pricing calculated for dealerships?

Plans include monthly SMS credits with volume discounts for dealership groups. MMS for vehicle photos and international rates vary by carrier.

Do you support two-way conversations with car buyers?

Yes. Route customer replies to sales, service, or financing departments, set response SLAs, and manage conversations across your dealership team.

Can I migrate from another automotive texting provider?

We'll help import customer data, vehicle preferences, opt-in records, and messaging templates—so your dealership transitions smoothly.
